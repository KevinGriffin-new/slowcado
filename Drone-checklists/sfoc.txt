


SFOC
----

Operations requiring an SFOC

☐	Any drone weighing more that 25Kg
☐	Flying beyond Visual Line of Sight (VLOS)
☐	Pilot who is not a Canadian citizen or permanent resident of Canada
☐	Flying higher than normal maximum altitudes unless authorized by Air Traffic Control
☐	Operating more than 5 RPAs from one control station
☐	Flying at a special aviation event or at an advertised event
☐	Carrying hazardous payload
☐	Flying within 3 nautical miles of a military aerodrome
☐	Any other operation that the Minister decides requires an SFOC
