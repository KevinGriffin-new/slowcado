


Landing Procedure
-----------------

Landing Procedure

☐	Alert crew and bystanders of approaching aircraft
☐	Ensure landing site is clear of debris and is suitable for landing
☐	When drone is approximately 10m away, rotate drone to face away (to allow
	natural flight control movements)
☐	Ensure all crew and involved people are clear of landing site
☐	Ensure all bystanders are 30m away from landing site
☐	Land drone
☐	Turn off motors and camera
☐	Retrieve drone and inspect for damage
☐	Disassemble as required, apply gimbal guard and lens cover, stow gear
☐	Record flight in flight log
